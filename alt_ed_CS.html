Alt Ed

By using AltEd, discover the right alternative education for you and your career passion!

Project Overview

I was put in a team of three and given a project to develop a unique mobile app with a deadline of three weeks. We decided to design an app where it helps users to find the right alternative education for them. As a team we all contributed to each step of the design process. One of the most prominent roles I played was to find interviewees and conduct interviews with them, research on competitors, create value proposition, bring cohesiveness to the layout design, and lastly, bring all the deliverables together for presentation.

The Problem

Alternative education is a great way to enhace professional skills and to prepare oneself for the real world within a short period of time. However, there are not many resources that users can utilize to discover alternative education, making it difficult for individuals to pursue their careers. The goal is to help those trying to improve their lives by finding their passion and matching them with appropriate programs to advance their professional skills through our app AltEd.
